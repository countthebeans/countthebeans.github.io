Count The Beans is the business Teresa spent over a decade building the expertise to create. With a background spanning accounting firms, bookkeeping practices, and more than ten years of hands-on experience across construction and trades, retail and hospitality, health and wellness, and professional services — Teresa has seen every variation of a messy set of books. And fixed them.

That breadth of experience matters. It means Count The Beans doesn't apply a one-size-fits-all approach. It means your books are handled by someone who actually understands your industry — not just the software.

Why Count The Beans exists.

After years of watching clients get overcharged, under-informed, and left to figure out their finances alone — Teresa decided to do it differently. Count The Beans was founded on a straightforward idea: small business owners deserve a bookkeeper who tells them the truth, charges them fairly, and actually explains what's going on.

No hidden fees. No jargon designed to make you feel confused. No being passed around between staff. Just one experienced bookkeeper, completely across your file, every single month.

Count The Beans is based in Perth, Western Australia and works with sole traders and small businesses across the city — because great bookkeeping shouldn't only be available to big businesses with big budgets.

Privacy Policy

Count The Beans is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). Here's a plain-English summary of our key commitments.

What we collect

Contact details, financial and tax information, payroll records, and business information necessary to provide our services.

Why we collect it

To provide bookkeeping, payroll, and BAS/IAS services, meet our legal obligations, communicate with you, and issue invoices.

Who we share it with

We do not sell or trade your information. We may share with the ATO (for lodgements), Xero, and professional advisers where required by law.

AI tools

AI is used for drafting and administrative tasks only. No raw client data, TFNs, or sensitive personal information is entered into third-party AI platforms. AI is never used to lodge returns without human review.

Overseas contractors

Qualified overseas contractors may assist with bookkeeping tasks. All are bound by strict confidentiality agreements. No TFNs or sensitive identification data is shared. All work is reviewed by Count The Beans before delivery.

Data security & retention

Data is stored securely on encrypted cloud platforms. Records are retained for a minimum of 5 years as required by law.

Your rights

You may request access to or correction of your personal information at any time. Email teresa@countthebeans.com.au with subject line "Privacy Access Request".

Privacy enquiries: teresa@countthebeans.com.au — subject "Privacy Complaint". Unresolved complaints may be referred to the OAIC. Full policy available on request.
